Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 16, 2025

TL;DR - Your Privacy First:

  • SAR is a self-hosted application - your data stays on YOUR infrastructure
  • We do NOT collect, access, or store any of your financial data
  • All data processing happens in YOUR browser and YOUR database
  • No analytics, no tracking, no third-party data sharing

1. Introduction

SAR (Standalone Reporting) ("we", "our", "the Software") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SAR handles your data when you use the Software.

Key Principle: SAR is designed as a self-hosted, privacy-first application. We have no access to your data because you control where it's stored.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

SAR does NOT collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your financial data from QuickBooks
  • Your session/event data
  • Your Supabase credentials or database contents
  • Your QuickBooks OAuth tokens
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • IP addresses or device information
  • Cookies or tracking data

3. How SAR Works (Data Flow)

3.1 Self-Hosted Architecture

SAR runs entirely on YOUR infrastructure:

  • Frontend: Runs in your browser (locally or on your server)
  • Database: Your Supabase instance (you control access and backups)
  • No Backend Server: SAR has no central server that collects data

3.2 QuickBooks Integration

When you connect SAR to QuickBooks:

  • OAuth authentication happens directly between YOUR browser and QuickBooks
  • Access tokens are stored in YOUR browser's localStorage
  • Data fetched from QuickBooks goes directly to YOUR browser, then to YOUR Supabase database
  • SAR developers have NO access to your QuickBooks data or tokens

3.3 Data Storage

All your data is stored in:

  • Your Supabase Database: You control access, security, and retention policies
  • Browser localStorage: Credentials and tokens stored locally on your device

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Supabase

You use Supabase to store your data. Supabase's privacy policy applies to data stored there. Review Supabase's privacy policy at: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.2 QuickBooks (Intuit)

When you authorize SAR to access QuickBooks, you're granting read-only access under OAuth 2.0. Intuit's privacy policy governs your QuickBooks data. Review Intuit's privacy policy at: https://www.intuit.com/privacy/

4.3 No Other Third Parties

SAR does NOT integrate with:

  • Analytics services (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Data brokers or aggregators
  • Social media platforms

5. Data Security

5.1 Your Responsibilities

Since SAR is self-hosted, YOU are responsible for:

  • Securing your Supabase credentials (URL and API keys)
  • Securing your QuickBooks OAuth tokens
  • Implementing access controls (who can use SAR)
  • Using HTTPS when deploying SAR to production
  • Regular backups of your Supabase database
  • Rotating credentials periodically

5.2 SAR's Security Features

SAR includes:

  • OAuth 2.0 with CSRF protection for QuickBooks integration
  • Read-only QuickBooks access (cannot modify your accounting data)
  • No hardcoded credentials in source code
  • Client-side only architecture (no data leaves your control)

5.3 Production Recommendations

For production deployments:

  • Enable Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase
  • Use HTTPS (not HTTP)
  • Consider encrypting sensitive fields in your database
  • Implement authentication/authorization for SAR access
  • Review Supabase audit logs regularly

6. Data Retention

You control data retention. SAR does not automatically delete any data. You decide:

  • How long to keep session data
  • How long to keep expense records
  • When to archive old reports
  • When to revoke QuickBooks access

To delete data, simply delete it from your Supabase database or revoke QuickBooks OAuth access from your QuickBooks account settings.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

SAR does NOT share your data with anyone because we don't have access to it.

Your data may be accessible to:

  • You and authorized users you grant access to
  • Supabase (as your database provider - see their privacy policy)
  • QuickBooks/Intuit (they already have your accounting data)

SAR will never:

  • Sell your data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use your data for machine learning or AI training
  • Provide data to third parties (we can't - we don't have it!)

8. Your Data Rights

Because you control where SAR data is stored, you have full rights to:

  • Access: View all your data directly in Supabase
  • Export: Export data from Supabase at any time
  • Delete: Delete records from your database whenever you want
  • Modify: Edit or correct any data in your database
  • Revoke: Disconnect QuickBooks or Supabase at any time
  • Portability: Your data is in a PostgreSQL database (industry standard)

9. Children's Privacy

SAR is a business application not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Users

SAR can be deployed anywhere. Your data residency depends on where YOU choose to host:

  • Supabase allows you to choose your database region (US, EU, etc.)
  • QuickBooks data is stored by Intuit according to their policies
  • SAR itself stores no data centrally

11. Compliance

11.1 GDPR Compliance

If you process EU personal data, you are the data controller. SAR does not process personal data on behalf of anyone (we have no access to your data). You should:

  • Configure Supabase in an EU region if required
  • Implement appropriate security measures
  • Provide privacy notices to your employees/users

11.2 CCPA Compliance

SAR does not "sell" personal information because we don't collect or have access to it.

12. Open Source

SAR is designed to be transparent. You can review the source code to verify:

  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No tracking or analytics code
  • All data processing happens client-side

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of SAR after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy or SAR's data practices, please contact your system administrator or the person who provided you with SAR.

Remember: SAR developers have no access to your data and cannot assist with data requests. Contact your Supabase administrator for data access or deletion requests.

15. Summary

Privacy in Plain English:

SAR is like a calculator app - it runs on your device, processes your data locally, and stores results where YOU tell it to. We (SAR developers) never see your numbers, never track your usage, and have no central server collecting anything.

Your data. Your database. Your control. Zero tracking.
